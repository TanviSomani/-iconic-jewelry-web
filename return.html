Return Policy

NOTICE: Due to Covid-19, shipping times are slower than usual. Please be patient. We have temporarily switched our shipping carrier. All domestic orders over $100 will receive free shipping. If you are local to Vancover we do offer FREE in-store pick up.

We will happily exchange or issue store credit on almost all products with ALL original tags still attached within 10 days of the confirmation of delivery. The receipt included in your package must be present.

*Please note: items marked Final Sale cannot be exchanged.

To begin the exchange process please contact us via Email: appliedresearch2021@gmail.com or Phone: + 01 234 567 89.

Exchanges

All exchanges must meet the following requirements.

* Merchandise exchange process must be initiated within 10 days of receiving order.

* Merchandise must be undamaged.

* Merchandise must have all tags, accessories, and original paperwork.

* Final Sale and Special Order items may not be returned.

If any returns do not meet these requirements, you will be contacted, the items will be returned to you immediately, and a refund will not be issued. We reserve the right to refuse a refund if the items have any signs of wear, alterations, misuse or damage. Please contact our customer service if you have any questions: appliedresearch2021@gmail.com

Please include the following when emailing us for exchanges.

- Name

- Phone Number

- Order Number

- Item(s) being exchanged

- Reason
